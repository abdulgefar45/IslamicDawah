What is Islam?

Understanding the message of peace, submission to God, and complete way of life

Meaning of Islam

Islam means "submission to the will of God." It's a monotheistic religion revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) through the Quran. Muslims believe in one God (Allah) and that Muhammad is His final messenger.

Five Pillars

1. Shahadah (Faith declaration)
2. Salah (Five daily prayers)
3. Zakat (Charity to the poor)
4. Sawm (Fasting in Ramadan)
5. Hajj (Pilgrimage to Mecca)

Core Beliefs

• Belief in One God (Allah)
• Belief in all Prophets from Adam to Muhammad
• Belief in Holy Books (Quran, Torah, Gospel)
• Belief in Angels
• Belief in Day of Judgment
• Belief in Divine Decree
